This page was set up as a simple front end challenge for HacktoberFest 2021 event.

The goal is to create your personal pet or favourite animal card, style it any way you'd like to and have fun making it.

Before you start, please have a look at a couple of participation rules I've set up. Not following the set rules might get your pull request rejected.

Will Be Accepted:
  • A line or two of introduction text about your pet or favourite animal
  • Any links to pointing towards more information on the chosen animal
  • Any personal links to your portfolios, projects or social media
  • Any personally owned images or videos (only via embeds)
Won't Be Accepted:
  • Submittions containing offensive text, images, videos and links of any kind
  • Use of copyrighted or promotional content not owned by yourself
  • Use of auto play for any videos submitted
  • Use of copy/pasted materials
